Sahyadri Hub (Tourism & Trek Companion)

Sahyadri Hub is a comprehensive, feature-rich Android mobile application built for trekkers, adventurers, and tourists exploring the Sahyadri mountain ranges (including Harishchandragad, Aadrai Jungle, Kalsubai, and Malshej Ghat). The application organizes route details, weather safety metrics, verified local trek guides, authentic home-stay accommodations, traditional local dining outlets, interactive trekking navigation maps, and direct emergency rescue contacts.

Key Features

  • Trek & Fort Explorer: Comprehensive catalog of Sahyadri forts and trekking trails with route maps and difficulty ratings
  • Stay & Food Finder: Search and book verified local home-stays and order traditional Maharashtrian cuisine
  • Trek Guide Booking: Hire local guides with verification details to assist on challenging trails
  • Emergency Support Hub: Instant access to nearby rescue groups, local hospital contacts, and weather warning alerts
