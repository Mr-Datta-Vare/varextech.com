Hotel Kalamjai (Khireshwar Stay & Guide)

Hotel Kalamjai is a full-featured travel, lodging, and local dining website designed for guests visiting Malshej Ghat and Harishchandragad base village, Khireshwar. The portal allows travelers to browse comfortable single rooms and camping tents, review authentic local wood-fired Maharashtrian cuisine (Chulivarcha Jevan), get guidance on Kalu Waterfall and Aadrai Jungle treks, and make instant bookings via WhatsApp integration.

Key Features

  • Stay & Cabin Packages: Interactive cards showing Single Room and Sharing Tent packages with price ranges
  • Monsoon Attractions Guide: Informative hubs detailing local attractions like Malshej Ghat, Harishchandragad, Aadrai, and Kalu Waterfall
  • Structured Schema Markup: Rich JSON-LD local business SEO tags to boost local search rankings
  • Trekking Guide Hire Portal: Contact details and guidance to hire verified local trek guides for safety
